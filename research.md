## Research Areas

My research focuses on Advanced Air Mobility (AAM), aviation systems integration, airport operations, GIS-based spatial analysis, and transportation sustainability.

### Current Topics

- AAM integration at large U.S. hub airports
- Airport operational readiness
- Aviation safety and risk analysis
- GIS-based decision support
- Sustainability assessment frameworks
- Human–automation interaction
- Vertiport planning and infrastructure
