I am **Mabior Deng Kuer Mabior**, a PhD student in **Aviation and Aerospace** at Purdue University.

My research focuses on **Advanced Air Mobility (AAM)**, airport systems, aviation safety, GIS-based decision support, sustainability, and transportation systems analysis. I am interested in how emerging aviation systems can be integrated into existing airport environments while balancing safety, operational efficiency, infrastructure readiness, and social sustainability.

## Background

I hold a **BEng in Aerospace Engineering** and an **MSc in Aviation Engineering**. My academic and research background spans aviation safety, airport systems, airspace operations, GIS and spatial analytics, sustainability assessment, and emerging aerospace technologies.
