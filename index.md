## About

I am Mabior Deng Kuer Mabior, a PhD student in Aviation and Aerospace at Purdue University. My research focuses on Advanced Air Mobility (AAM), airport systems, aviation safety, GIS-based decision support, sustainability, and transportation systems analysis.

My work examines how emerging aviation systems can be integrated into existing airport environments while balancing safety, operational efficiency, infrastructure readiness, and social sustainability.

---

## Background

I hold a BEng in Aerospace Engineering and an MSc in Aviation Engineering. My academic and research background spans aviation safety, airport systems, airspace operations, GIS and spatial analytics, sustainability assessment, and emerging aerospace technologies.

My current work explores airport-centric frameworks for evaluating the integration of Advanced Air Mobility systems into large U.S. hub airports. Research areas include:

- Vertiport infrastructure planning
- Airspace integration
- GIS-based decision-support systems
- Human factors and automation
- Operational readiness for AAM deployment
